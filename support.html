Common Questions

Quick answers before you reach out.

From the home screen, tap the plus icon. Choose your workout duration, style (HIIT, EMOM, Tabata, etc.), and focus area (full body, upper, lower, core). FireTime will generate a complete workout with exercises — ready in about 10 seconds.

Weights mode is designed for gym sessions. Tap the plus icon and select "Weights" as your structure. Pick your exercises (up to 8), and set your rest time between exercises (60s, 90s, or 120s). Each exercise is an open interval — work at your own pace and tap Done when you're finished.

An open interval has no countdown — you work at your own pace and tap Done when you're ready to move on. This is useful for strength training where you want to control your tempo. You can add open intervals to any custom workout by setting the duration to 0.

Tap the pause button during any workout to pause the timer. Tap again to resume. On Apple Watch, swipe right to access pause and end controls.

Tap on any saved workout to open it in the workout builder, where you can edit intervals, durations, and names. To delete a workout, swipe left on the workout card from the home screen and tap Delete.

Long-press on any workout card and drag it to your preferred position. Your custom order is saved automatically.

Yes! FireTime includes a companion Apple Watch app. Start a workout on your iPhone and it syncs to your watch, or start directly from your watch. You'll see the timer, current exercise, and upcoming intervals right on your wrist.

When you complete a workout, FireTime can log it to Apple Health (with your permission). This includes workout duration, estimated calories burned, and heart rate data if you're wearing an Apple Watch.

Troubleshooting

If you have an Apple Watch, FireTime uses your Watch's sensors for accurate heart rate and calorie data. For this to work, you need to grant Health permissions on both your iPhone and your Watch. On your Watch, go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → FireTime and make sure Active Calories and Heart Rate are enabled.

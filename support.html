Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a quick workout? +
From the home screen, tap "Quick Build". Choose your workout duration, style (HIIT, EMOM, Tabata, etc.), and focus area (full body, upper, lower, core). FireTime will generate a complete workout with exercises — ready in about 10 seconds.
Can I create my own custom workouts? +
Yes! Tap "New Workout" to open the workout builder. You can add intervals, set work and rest times, name your exercises, and save your workout for future use. Your custom workouts are stored locally on your device.
Does FireTime work with Apple Watch? +
Yes! FireTime includes a companion Apple Watch app. Start a workout on your iPhone and it syncs to your watch, or start directly from your watch. You'll see the timer, current exercise, and upcoming intervals right on your wrist.
How does Apple Health integration work? +
When you complete a workout, FireTime can log it to Apple Health (with your permission). This includes workout duration, estimated calories burned, and heart rate data if you're wearing an Apple Watch. You can enable or disable this in Settings.
Do I need to create an account? +
No. FireTime works completely offline and doesn't require any account, login, or personal information. Just download and start training.
Can I use FireTime without an Apple Watch? +
Absolutely. The Apple Watch app is optional. FireTime works perfectly on iPhone alone with audio cues and haptic feedback to guide you through your workout.
How do I change the audio or haptic settings? +
Go to Settings in the app. You can adjust voice announcements, sound effects, and haptic feedback. You can also choose different countdown sounds and toggle the "3-2-1" countdown announcement.
My workouts disappeared. How do I get them back? +
Workouts are stored locally on your device. If you deleted and reinstalled the app, or got a new device, the workouts won't transfer automatically. We recommend taking screenshots of important custom workouts as a backup. A cloud sync feature may be added in a future update.
Is FireTime free? +
Yes, FireTime is free to download and use. There are no ads, no subscriptions, and no hidden fees. All features are available to everyone.
How do I report a bug or request a feature? +
Email us at firetime.app@gmail.com. We read every message and appreciate your feedback. Bug reports help us improve, and feature requests shape the future of the app.

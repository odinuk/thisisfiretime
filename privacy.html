Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

FireTime is built with a simple philosophy: your data is yours. We don't collect it, we don't store it, and we don't sell it.

What We Collect

Nothing. FireTime does not collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics. The app runs entirely on your device.

Apple Health Integration

FireTime can sync your workouts to Apple Health if you grant permission. This data:

  • Is stored only on your device and in your personal iCloud (if you use iCloud for Health)
  • Is never transmitted to FireTime or any third party
  • Can be deleted at any time through the Apple Health app
  • Requires your explicit permission before any data is written

We request write-only access to Apple Health to log your workouts. We do not read any of your health data.

No Account Required

FireTime does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use the app.

No Tracking

FireTime does not use any analytics services, tracking pixels, or advertising identifiers. We have no idea how you use the app — and we prefer it that way.

No Advertising

FireTime does not display advertisements and does not share any information with advertising networks.

Local Storage

Your custom workouts and preferences are stored locally on your device. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not backed up to any external servers
  • Is deleted if you uninstall the app

Third-Party Services

FireTime does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. The only external integration is Apple Health, which is entirely controlled by Apple and your device settings.

Children's Privacy

FireTime does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Given our commitment to not collecting data, we don't anticipate significant changes.
